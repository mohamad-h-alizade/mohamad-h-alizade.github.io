I am an Electrical Engineer. I graduated from Sharif University of Technology (SUT) in 2022 with a focus on machine learning and signal processing. After completing my degree, I worked with Odense University Hospital (OUH) for six months. Supervised by Prof. Manouchehr Vafaee, I processed brain signals which sparked my interest in graph-based models. Since then, I have been part of a research group at SUT. Here I collabrate with Dr. Aref Einizade on the clustering of graph signals. You can read my CV here.

Research: I work in the areas of graph learning and information processing. In the emerging field of graph signal processing (GSP), graph learning is the inference of a graph's topology from observations on its nodes, i.e. graph signals. My research aims to unravel hidden relationships among data entities, which in return increases robustness, security and explainablity of downstream models. My current research centers on incorprating node-side information into the clustering of graph signals. I am also broadly interested in geometric deep learning, kernel methods, and the role of tensor decompositions in dimensionality reduction.
